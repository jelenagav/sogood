Our Story

At SoGood, we make condoms for happier/safe and world changing sex. Our condoms are made from natural rubber and are fair trade, vegan, eco-friendly, and nontoxic. SoGood stands for sexual health worldwide, which is why with every purchase you support sexual education in sub-saharan Africa. Make love while sharing love- interested? Stay tuned, we are launching soon.

You may be surprised to learn that most condoms are NOT vegan. This is because of the use of casein, a milk protein commonly found in traditional latex condoms. SoGood condoms do not contain any animal-by products and are produced at a slightly higer temperature with a specific formulation and ingredients that allow us to produce the condoms without casein. Our products are also cruelty-free, whidh means that SoGood does not test on animals. Our condoms are compliamt with EU and ISO standards and thus meet the same safety standards as non-vegan condoms, which means that—when used correctly— they are 98% effective in preventing pregnancy and STDs.

SoGood condoms are eco-friendly. The latex used in SoGood condoms comes from sustainably grown rubber trees, indigenous to the region where it grows. We always look to be as sustainable as possible throughout our supply chain, from sourcing and harvesting to the manufacturing proces. As SoGood is committed to keeping our carbon footprint low, we are committed to move our production facility (Germany) and sourcing location (Malaysia) within closer proximity of one another within the next two years. SoGood's packaging is made from recycled materials and vegetable inks, no wasteful cellophane or plastics are involved.

With every purchase at SoGood you support sexual education in sub-saharan Africa. We partner with NGOs alliance for Reproductive Health, NGO focused on grassroots development in West Africa, works together with community-based associations to achieve their development objectives, including green clinics.

SoGood condoms are fair trade, which means that the latex used for our condoms has been fairly sourced and traded. We believe that fair wages and safe working conditions are a basic human right and make sure that the people who contribute to our products receive a fair living wage, work reasonable hours under fair labor conditions.
