Worldwide, more than 1 million sexually transmitted infections are acquired every day (WHO). Up to 80% of these infections occur in less developed countries, where awareness of STIs is lower and people have limited access to condoms and effective treatment options (the World Bank). Left untreated, STIs can cause serious long-term health consequences, as well as perinatal and neonatal death associated with HIV and Syphilis. These complications are exponentially worse in low income countries.

Not only is that not OK, it is also preventable. That’s why for every purchase you make at SoGood, we donate 10% to local partners in Sub-Saharan Africa who are working to improve health education and access to sexual and reproductive health services. We are committed doing our bit to make the world a better and more equal place and will increase our donations once SoGood becomes profitable.

Vegan

You may be surprised to learn that most condoms are NOT vegan. This is because of the use of casein, a milk protein commonly found in traditional latex condoms. SoGood condoms do not contain casein or any other animal-by products. Our products are also cruelty-free, whidh means that SoGood does not test on animals.

Sustainable

Our condoms are eco-friendly. The latex used in SoGood condoms is sourced from sustainably grown rubber trees, indigenous to the region where it grows. We always look to be as sustainable as possible throughout our supply chain, from sourcing and harvesting to the manufacturing proces.

Fair trade

The latex used for our condoms has been fairly sourced and traded. We believe that fair wages and safe working conditions are a basic human right and make sure that the people who contribute to our products receive a fair living wage and work reasonable hours under fair labor conditions. As we grow and learn, our goal is to work towards a more inclusive supply chain for smallholder farmers.
